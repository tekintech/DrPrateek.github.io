About

Bridging Clinical
& Computational
Neuroscience

I am a medical student at AIIMS Rishikesh with a focused trajectory in neurology, neurodegeneration, and data-driven neuroscience. My research spans observational studies, systematic reviews, network meta-analyses, and large-scale bibliometric workflows.

An active contributor to international neurology collaborations, I have presented original research at AAN, EAN, WCN, AHA, and ESMO. I currently serve as Co-Student Editor-in-Chief of the International Journal of Medical Students (IJMS).

My technical stack includes R (bibliometrix, RevMan), Python, and bioinformatics pipelines. Trained in computational neuroscience through Neuromatch Academy and the Harvard-affiliated Aspire Leaders Program.
